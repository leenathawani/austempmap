Global Life Expectancy

Life Expectancy refers to the number of years a person is expected to live, and is an indication of the overall mortality level of the population. Longevity is affected by various factors such as gender, genetics, hygiene levels, access to healthcare, crime rate, etc. and can therefore be significantly different in different parts of the world. These visualisations depict the variation in life expectancy across the different countries and continents around the globe.
